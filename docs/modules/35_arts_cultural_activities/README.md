## MODULE OVERVIEW

**Name:** Arts & Cultural Activities Management Module  
**Role:** Creative Expression & Cultural Development - Artistic Excellence & Performance Platform  
**Type:** Critical Student Development & Cultural Module  
**Dependencies:** Integrates with Student Management, Events, Communication, Facilities modules  

**Primary Functions:**
- Music Programs - Vocal, instrumental, choir, orchestra, music theory
- Dance Programs - Classical (Bharatanatyam, Kathak), Western, Folk, Contemporary

- Visual Arts - Painting, sculpture, pottery, digital art, photography
- Cultural Events - Annual Function, cultural festivals, competitions
- Talent Shows - Inter-house, inter-school competitions
- Art Exhibitions - Student artwork displays, galleries
- Performance Tracking - Participation, achievements, awards
- Resource Management - Instruments, costumes, art supplies
- Portfolio Development - Student portfolios for college applications
