## MENTORSHIP PROGRAM

### Alumni-Student Mentorship

**Purpose:** Connect current students with alumni for career guidance, industry insights

**Structure:**
- **Mentors:** 200-300 alumni volunteers
- **Mentees:** 400-600 students (Grade 10-12)
- **Duration:** 6-12 months
- **Frequency:** Monthly meetings (virtual/in-person)
- **Topics:** Career planning, college selection, interview prep, industry insights

**Matching Process:**
1. **Student Application:**
   - Career interests, goals
   - Preferred industry, location
   - Preferred mentor characteristics
2. **Mentor Registration:**
   - Industry, expertise
   - Availability, time commitment
   - Mentoring experience
3. **Matching Algorithm:**
   - Match based on interests, industry, location
   - Consider mentor capacity (max 3 mentees)
   - Manual review by alumni relations team
4. **Introduction:**
   - Email introduction
   - Kick-off meeting scheduled
   - Mentorship agreement signed

**Success Metrics:**
- **Mentor Participation:** 250 alumni (2.5% of total)
- **Student Participation:** 500 students (28% of Grade 10-12)
- **Completion Rate:** 75% (complete 6-month program)
- **Satisfaction:** 4.5/5.0 (students), 4.3/5.0 (mentors)
- **Impact:** 80% students report improved career clarity

**Example:**
- **Mentor:** Mr. Rajesh Kumar (Batch 2010, Software Engineer at Google)
- **Mentee:** Aarav Sharma (Grade 11, interested in Computer Science)
- **Duration:** 6 months (Jan-Jun 2026)
- **Meetings:** 6 virtual meetings (1 per month)
- **Topics Covered:**
  - Computer Science career paths
  - College selection (IITs, NITs, private colleges)
  - Coding interview preparation
  - Internship opportunities
  - Day in the life of a software engineer
- **Outcome:** Aarav got internship at startup, improved coding skills, decided to pursue CS at IIT

**Planning Process:**
1. **Reunion Committee:**

2. **Date Selection:**
   - Weekend, avoid exam periods
   - Coordinate with school calendar

## OUTBOUND CONNECTIONS (Alumni → Other Modules)

### 1. TO DONATIONS MODULE

**WHY This Connection Exists:**
Alumni donations need to be tracked and managed. Alumni module sends donation data to Donations module.

**DATA FLOW:**
- **Donation Information:**
  - Donor name (alumni), batch year
  - Donation amount, date
  - Donation category (scholarship, infrastructure, annual fund)
  - Payment method, transaction ID
- **Tax Receipt:**
  - 80G certificate request
  - Donor PAN, address
  - Receipt generation

**TRIGGER EVENT:**
- **Donation Made:** Log donation, send to Donations module
- **Tax Receipt Requested:** Generate 80G certificate
- **Annual Report:** Include donor recognition

**IMPACT:**
- **Centralized Tracking:**
  - All donations tracked in one system
  - Financial reporting
  - Donor analytics
- **Tax Compliance:**
  - 80G certificates for tax deductions
  - Audit trail

- **Meeting 4 (15-Apr):** Internship opportunities
