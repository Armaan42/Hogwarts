## TRAINING METHODOLOGIES

### Skill Development Programs

**Beginner Level (Grades 6-8):**
- **Focus:** Basic skills, rules, sportsmanship
- **Duration:** 6 months
- **Frequency:** 3 days/week, 1 hour/session
- **Outcome:** Foundation skills, game understanding

**Intermediate Level (Grades 9-10):**
- **Focus:** Advanced skills, tactics, fitness
- **Duration:** 1 year
- **Frequency:** 4 days/week, 1.5 hours/session
- **Outcome:** Competitive readiness, team play

**Advanced Level (Grades 11-12):**
- **Focus:** Elite performance, strategy, mental toughness
- **Duration:** 2 years
- **Frequency:** 5-6 days/week, 2 hours/session
- **Outcome:** Inter-school, district, state level competition

---

### Coaching Certification Programs

**School's Coaching Standards:**
- **Minimum Qualification:** Level 1 certification in respective sport
- **Preferred:** Level 2 or higher
- **Continuing Education:** Annual workshops, seminars

**Coaching Staff Development:**
- **Annual Training:** All coaches attend 2-3 workshops per year
- **Peer Learning:** Monthly coaches' meetings
- **Performance Review:** Annual evaluation based on team performance

## MEDICAL & FITNESS TRACKING

### Pre-Participation Medical Examination

**Purpose:** Ensure athlete fitness, identify health risks

**Process:**
1. **Medical History:** Family history, past injuries, allergies
2. **Physical Examination:** Height, weight, BMI, blood pressure
3. **Fitness Tests:** Stamina, strength, flexibility
4. **Clearance:** Doctor certifies fitness for sports
5. **Valid For:** 1 year (annual renewal)

**Example:**
- **Athlete:** Rohan Sharma (Grade 10, Cricket)
- **Examination Date:** 01-Apr-2025
- **Height:** 170 cm, Weight: 60 kg, BMI: 20.8 (Normal)
- **Blood Pressure:** 120/80 (Normal)
- **Fitness Tests:** Excellent stamina, good strength
- **Clearance:** Fit for cricket (valid till 31-Mar-2026)
- **Recommendations:** Continue training, maintain fitness

---

### Injury Management

**Injury Reporting:**
1. **Immediate:** Coach reports injury during match/training
2. **First Aid:** On-site first aid provided
3. **Medical Assessment:** School doctor examines
4. **Treatment:** Rest, physiotherapy, medication
5. **Recovery:** Gradual return to training
6. **Clearance:** Doctor clears for full participation

**Example:**
- **Athlete:** Aarav Kumar (Football, Grade 11)
- **Injury:** Ankle sprain during match (15-Sep-2025)
- **First Aid:** Ice pack, compression bandage
- **Medical Assessment:** Grade 2 sprain, 4-6 weeks recovery
- **Treatment:** Rest (2 weeks), physiotherapy (4 weeks)
- **Recovery:** Gradual training (week 5-6)
- **Clearance:** Fit for full participation (20-Oct-2025)
- **Return:** Played next match (25-Oct-2025)
